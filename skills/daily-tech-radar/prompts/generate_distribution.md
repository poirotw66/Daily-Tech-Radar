# Distribution Copy Prompt

Generate platform-specific distribution copy from the reviewed article draft.

Outputs:
1. LinkedIn post
2. Threads sequence
3. Facebook group post
4. Newsletter summary
5. SEO metadata

Rules:
- Do not add new factual claims beyond the article.
- Keep LinkedIn professional and insight-led.
- Keep Threads concise and sequential.
- Keep Facebook group copy practical and less formal.
- Include URL placeholders only.
- Keep SEO metadata accurate and non-clickbait.

Return Markdown sections:
- `## LinkedIn`
- `## Threads`
- `## Facebook 社團`
- `## Newsletter`
- `## SEO Metadata`
