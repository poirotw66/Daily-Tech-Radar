# Draft Article Prompt

You are writing a Traditional Chinese technical article for the user's personal website.

Write with these principles:
- practical and clear
- technical but not academic
- opinionated but not exaggerated
- useful to engineers, PMs, and automation-focused readers
- include risks and limitations
- include one section called `我的判斷`
- include at least one practical application scenario

Do not merely translate or summarize sources. Explain why the topic matters and how it could be used in real workflows.

Rules:
- Cite source titles or URLs inline where concrete facts appear.
- Label interpretation as interpretation.
- Avoid unsupported future predictions.
- Avoid confidential workplace examples.
- For financial or enterprise applications, mention data, access control, auditability, governance, or compliance caveats.

Return Markdown only.
